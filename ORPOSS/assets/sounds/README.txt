Place your .wav sound files here. Required filenames:

  new_order.wav   — plays on kitchen & customer screen when a new order arrives
  order_placed.wav — plays on the POS terminal when PLACE ORDER is pressed
  serving.wav     — plays in kitchen when MARK SERVING is pressed
  claimed.wav     — plays in kitchen when MARK CLAIMED is pressed
  cancel.wav      — plays in kitchen when an order is cancelled
  ready.wav       — plays on customer screen when order moves to SERVING

Note: winsound only supports .wav files, not .mp3.
      Convert .mp3 to .wav at convertio.co or using Audacity.
